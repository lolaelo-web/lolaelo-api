Investor overview

Investor's corner

A fair-commission, curated OTA built for independent hotels and price sensitive travelers. Delaware-registered LLC.

Market: Philippines (strategic point of entry) Model: 5%–15% fair commissions Supply: Direct (not aggregated) Raise: $600k pre-seed (SAFE)

Problem

The OTA industry is structurally misaligned. Hotels face high margins and pay-for-play visibility, while travelers filter through cluttered, overpriced inventory.

Solution

Lolaelo is a curated OTA with fair commissions that improves hotel profitability and delivers lower prices through direct inventory and standardized, high-quality content.

  • Direct (not aggregated) inventory for cleaner economics
  • Fair commissions (5%–15%) to increase partner adoption and conversion
  • Consistent, media-verified content for better UX and faster purchase decisions
