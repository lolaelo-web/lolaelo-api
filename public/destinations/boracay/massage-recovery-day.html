How to structure the day

Treat this as a buffer day. Do not schedule tours or travel right after your massage.

  • Sleep in or take a calm beach walk.
  • Massage late morning or early afternoon.
  • Light meal and early night.

Choosing the right place

Hotel spas are quieter and more controlled. Local shops are cheaper and flexible. Decide what matters more today.

  • Hotel spa: comfort and privacy.
  • Local shop: value and convenience.
  • Beach massage: relaxing, but noisier.

Do not overbook

One solid session beats stacking treatments. Recovery works best when you actually rest afterward.

Hydrate and eat light

Water before and after matters more than the specific massage type. Keep meals simple so you do not undo the recovery.

FAQ

When is the best time to schedule a massage?

Late morning or mid afternoon keeps the rest of your day flexible.

Should I book in advance?

Hotel spas yes. Local shops usually accept walk ins.

Is it kid friendly?

Yes, especially if you go earlier and choose a quieter setting.
