The recovery protocol

1) Hydrate first. 2) Massage late morning or mid afternoon. 3) Eat light after. 4) Do not schedule a hard activity right after. 5) Sleep earlier than usual. This is how you actually feel the benefits tomorrow.

FAQ

When is the best time to schedule a massage in Boracay?

Late morning or mid afternoon works best. You avoid peak sun hours and still have energy for dinner later.

Should I book in advance?

For hotel spas, yes. For beachside or local shops, walk in is usually fine during the day.

Are massages in Boracay family friendly?

Many places are fine with kids nearby or offer shorter sessions. Ask before booking and choose quieter hours.
