Pick your lane

Choose one lane. If you try to do everything, you lose half a day.

Family lane

Keep it easy and predictable.

  • Food first
  • One activity
  • Short esplanade walk

Shopping lane

Come in with targets.

  • Two stores max
  • Budget cap
  • Exit clean

Rain lane

Use MOA to protect your day.

  • Food + coffee
  • Walk indoors
  • Call it early

Esplanade short loop

A quick outdoor walk is enough. Do not turn it into a long mission.

Indoor reset

Aircon, food, and predictable logistics. Then you leave and keep your trip moving.

Protocol (so this does not eat your day)

  1. Arrive with a lane: family, shopping, or rain
  2. Set a time cap before you enter
  3. Do food early so decisions do not pile up later
  4. Exit clean once your goal is achieved
