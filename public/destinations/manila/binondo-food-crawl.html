Simple crawl plan (3 phases)

Phase 1

Anchor dish

Start with one must-have. Set the tone. Do not over-order.

Phase 2

Two savory stops

Split dishes. Keep portions small. Walk between stops.

Phase 3

Sweet or light finish

End clean. Dessert or tea. Then stop.

Street food discipline

One bite each. Share. Move on. This keeps the crawl alive.

Follow clusters, not hype

If three good places are close, that is your lane.

Common mistakes

  • Ordering full portions everywhere
  • Adding “just one more stop” too late
  • Skipping water and shade

Reality check

  • Binondo rewards pacing
  • Stopping early is a win
  • You can always come back another day
