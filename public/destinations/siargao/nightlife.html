FAQ

What is the safest way to get around at night?

Plan transport before you go out. Avoid risky late rides. If you are drinking, do not improvise transport decisions.

How late should we stay out?

Long enough to enjoy it, short enough to still feel good tomorrow. Most people win with a 2 to 4 hour night.

Is nightlife worth it if we are surfing early?

Only if you keep it controlled. One spot, early end, hydrate. Otherwise you pay for it the next morning.

What ruins the night?

No food, no ride plan, and too many stops. Keep it simple and the night stays fun.
