Do this if...

You want an easy, high payoff moment with almost no effort. You want to slow down for 60 minutes and let the day land.

Skip this if...

You are trying to cram in too many stops. You are already running late and you will be stressed the whole time.

FAQ

Do I need to plan this in advance?

No. Just decide the spot earlier in the day and commit. The planning is minimal. The execution is the whole thing.

What is the biggest mistake?

Chasing multiple spots and arriving late everywhere. Pick one and do it well.

Is sunset good for families?

Yes. It is one of the easiest, lowest-effort wins. Keep it early and pair it with dinner.

What should we do after sunset?

Dinner nearby. If you go out after, keep transport safe and avoid risky late rides.
