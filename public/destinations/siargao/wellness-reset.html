Protocol (simple, effective)

1) Do one recovery session. 2) Drink water. 3) Eat early. 4) Screen down. 5) Sleep. That is it. The next day will feel better.

Yoga is the cleanest reset

If you feel tight from surf and travel, yoga fixes you fast. The goal is mobility, not performance.

Goal: feel better tomorrow
Skip: ego sessions

FAQ

When should I schedule a reset day?

After two stacked days (surf + trip + heat) or whenever you feel worn down. One reset day prevents a bad final stretch.

What if I only want something quick?

Do the quick reset lane: one class or a short massage, then dinner and sleep. Simple and effective.

Do I need to book ahead?

If you want a specific time slot, yes. If you are flexible, you can often book same day.

What is the biggest mistake?

Calling it a rest day, then still stacking activities. If you want the benefit, commit to the reset.
