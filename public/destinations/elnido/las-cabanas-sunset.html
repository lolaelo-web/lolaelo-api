What to expect

Las Cabanas is popular because it is simple: sand, drinks, and a clean sunset line. The only thing that ruins it is showing up late and fighting for space.

What to do (simple plan)

  • Arrive early: give yourself breathing room.
  • Pick one base: don’t bounce between spots.
  • Keep it light: one drink, one snack, done.
  • Leave clean: don’t wait for peak exit chaos.

What to avoid

  • Arriving last minute and expecting a perfect view.
  • Turning it into a multi-stop crawl.
  • Over-ordering and spending the sunset in a transaction.
  • Staying too long and getting stuck in the exit rush.

Show up early, win the whole thing

This is not a place where late arrivals get rewarded. Early is the cheat code.

Keep it simple on purpose

One clean sunset beats a messy plan. Don’t optimize it into a problem.

FAQ

How early should I arrive?

Early enough that you are not stressed. If you show up already rushing, you missed the point.

Is this a full night out?

It can be, but it doesn’t need to be. The best version is simple: sunset, light food, leave clean.

What is the most common mistake?

Arriving late and trying to force a perfect view. If you want the view, you plan for it.
