Moalboal sardine run + snorkeling

Moalboal Sardine Run and Snorkeling

One of the highest payoff-to-effort experiences in Cebu. No boats required, no certification needed, and still genuinely impressive if done calmly.

Phase 2 Low barrier Underwater

How this works

The sardines gather just off the shoreline in Moalboal. You swim out, drop below the surface, and the school moves around you. No chasing, no touching, no drama.

Readiness

  • Comfortable swimmer
  • Basic mask and snorkel
  • Ability to stay calm underwater

Blueprint

  • Arrive early morning for calm water
  • Enter from Panagsama Beach
  • Float first, then duck dive gently

What good looks like

Slow movements, steady breathing, and letting the school pass around you.

Common mistakes

Chasing the fish, splashing, or crowding other swimmers.

When to skip

Rough seas, poor visibility, or if you are already exhausted from a long south Cebu day.
