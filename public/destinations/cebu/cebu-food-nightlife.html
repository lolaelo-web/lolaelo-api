Decision strips

Cebu’s food scene is broad. The mistake is trying to do everything in one night. Pick a lane and stop.

Local staples

Lechon, grilled meats, and simple comfort food. Heavy, satisfying, early finish.

Casual bars

Rooftops and neighborhood spots. Social without turning the night into a mission.

Late energy

Clusters like Mango Square. Commit only if the next day is light.
