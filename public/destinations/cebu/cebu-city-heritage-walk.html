What this walk gives you

Cebu City’s heritage zone is compact. You can see the core sites without turning it into a full-day commitment. That matters because Cebu rewards energy management.

Magellan’s Cross

Quick stop, heavy symbolism. See it, understand it, move on.

Basilica del Santo Niño

The emotional center of Cebu. Respectful pacing goes a long way.

Fort San Pedro

Small fort, clear context. Easy add-on without fatigue.

Colon Street

Oldest street in the country. Controlled chaos, best taken briefly.
